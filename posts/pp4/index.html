All for One and One (Space) for All! MCM/ICM Mock Competition

Objective

This project is practice for the MCM/ICM math modeling competition held annually. My team decided to tackle a previous problem that was highlighted as an option in the 2022 Interdisciplinary Contest in Modeling. This problem was looking into the future and tackling a problem that may impact global equity in the future: Asteroid Mining. Since no data was presented to analyze, my team had the challenging task to create our own data using our intuition on how the asteroid mining process would look in the future. We created models that took many things into account to create policies that would help keep each country's contribution to global equity around the same percentage pre asteroid mining practices. Also, we took a look into simulating the effects of asteroid mining over several years to see the long-time effects of such practices.

Software used: Google Docs, Google Sheets, SQL Server Management Studio (SSMS), Tableau
